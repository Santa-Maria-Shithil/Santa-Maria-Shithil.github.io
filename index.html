I am Santa Maria Shithil, a third-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Stony Brook University, New York. Currently, I am working on Sustainability Aware Software Systems (SASSY) design project with Professor Shuai Mu. Specifically, my project goal is to reduce the energy consumption of the distributed systems. I am also co-advised by Professor Anshul Gandhi, and Erez Zadok. My research interests lie in the intersection of distributed systems, and computer networks, and I plan to do some works in these areas.
